### Narrative Highlights

- **Act I (Setup)** introduces the Arcane Guild, its internal politics, and the inciting incident—the theft of the guild ledger and the whisper of a “Severed Veil.”
- **Act II (Confrontation)** takes Elara from the guild’s halls into a city of ruins, where she learns the Veil is a sentient corruption that feeds on guild magic. The conflict escalates with betrayals (Aric, Kael) and a deepening exploration of dark magic.
- **Act III (Resolution)** culminates in a climactic battle inside the Veil itself. Elara must merge loyalty and power, sacrifice the old order, and open a new chapter for magic and the guild.
